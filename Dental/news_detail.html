3D CONE BEAM CT scanner

Cập nhật: 06/05/2013

Computed tomography (Computed tomography-CT) or commonly known as tomography according to density (computed axial tomography-CAT) is the use of rotating the X-ray beam in conjunction with a computer program to make images of the body. Use the CT tomography allows shooting through the organs and tissues in the body through the techniques used different images. Figure CT tomography unique ability to provide clear images of soft tissue, bone, muscle, blood vessels without losing clarity.

The CT scans were performed within 10 seconds scanning X-ray beam and absolutely no pain for the patient. After the CT scan is done, the experts can get the computer image showing the full range of perspectives on the surface of bone, teeth and the details of the different perspectives on the 3-dimensional color images .

Currently CT has also been put into widespread use in the medical field. In particular, in dental imaging systems cone beam 3D (3D ConeBeam The Imaging system) was put into widespread use in the clinic.



Imaging systems cone beam 3D (3D Cone Beam Imaging The system)

3D imaging systems cone beam scanner is basically digital made by the X-ray beam cone is carefully controlled mounted on a rotating arm.

Patients were seated in a chair while the machine scans a complete circle around the head, gather the data needed to scan. The expert will then be able to get the data on your computer as 2D, 3D, panoramic images in sequential mouth. The images were reviewed from all angles, different magnifications, the relationship between the bones, teeth, respiratory, tissue, nerves from that assessment and plan appropriate treatment. Especially in the system produces high-quality images (CAT scan) of the emitted radiation are at their lowest.


 

CT images of patients was performed at EliteCT is used for the purpose of diagnosis and treatment planning of treatment for high accuracy in terms of image, and full details can help the doctor can diagnosis or treatment plan the most accurate way. Some treatment need using CBCT as endodontic with complex roots, bone grafting, implants placed, surveying the ground teeth, teeth stuck, ...

Dental Elite are currently using 3D imaging systems cone beam in the diagnosis, treatment planning for patients or consider other medical conditions. The performance takes place relatively quickly, not invasive and completely painless. Cone Beam CT imaging time faster than traditional CT films, but longer than the conventional dental films.
