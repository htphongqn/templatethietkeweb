Choosing a Shipping Method

You can choose your preferred shipping method on the Order Information page during the checkout process. For most products, Metwasecret.com will offer you a choice of two shipping methods on the Order Information page at checkout. Note that if every item in your order has the Free Shipping sticker, you won't need to select shipping – Metwasecret.com will automatically ship through the best method based on the order size and destination. Our two available shipping methods are:

Expedited Shipping
 

Our fastest option ships to all major destinations in 5 - 8 business days. All Expedited Shipping orders are eligible for shipping discounts according to package size and destination.

Standard Shipping
 

The most affordable option ships to all major destinations in 10 - 20 business days. All standard shipping orders are eligible for shipping discounts according to package size and destination.

Before we can ship your order, Metwasecret.com has to prepare your products, do strict quality-control tests and carefully package items. The duration of preparing your items for shipping and the delivery time is the total time to receive your order.

Qualifying for Free Shipping

Look out for these promotional stickers on our products to enjoy even greater savings:

Items with the Free Shipping sticker have zero shipping costs regardless of item quantity or your location. We choose the shipping method best suited to the package size and shipping destination. Please note that we will automatically ship packages via Super Saver Shipping (10 - 20 business days) if:
•    Your order contains faucets, grills, tents, oil paintings, chandeliers or MiniInTheBox items. 
•    Your order totals less than US$50.
•    Your order weighs over 2 kg.

Calculating Shipping Costs

Shipping costs depend on the number of items and the weight of your order, as well as the shipping methodshipping method chosen. The prices displayed on product information pages do not include shipping costs.

Follow these steps to calculate shipping costs on any order:


•    On the item's Product Details page, click on Add to Cart.

•    Enter your Shopping Cart and look for “Estimate Shipping Costs” at the bottom of the page to see total shipping costs for the items in your cart.

•    On the Ship to: drop-down menu, choose the country of delivery for a more accurate calculation of your shipping costs.
You can open your Shopping Cart at any time by clicking the Cart at the top of every page on Metwasecret.com.

Putting an item in your cart doesn't mean you have to order it. If you no longer want an item after seeing the estimated shipping costs, click Delete

Return Policy

At Metwasecret.com, returns are easy! We strive to offer the best shopping experience, including fast and hassle-free returns. Please contact our Customer Service to process your return.

Do I need to send products all the way to your Overseas Factory?

Australian customers can return their products to our Australian warehouse, Customers from other countries will need to send their products to our warehouse in Australia.

The products are fine, but I changed my mind

Products with no quality-related issues can be returned within 30 days of receiving your order. 

For Made-To-Order clothing, if the items were made accurately according to your order specifications but do not fit, you are eligible for one free resizing service. You will be responsible for all shipping fees. For customized wedding dresses a free lifetime alteration service is available.

Any product that has been used does not qualify for a return. Returned items must be unused, with original tags and packaging.

Products with quality problems

We guarantee all of our products against material and workmanship defects within the warranty period listed in the table below. If a product is not listed, the warranty period is 30 days. If a defect arises and a valid claim is received by Customer Service within the warranty period, we can offer you an exchange, repair service, or new components for replacement. We will be responsible for all shipping fees.
