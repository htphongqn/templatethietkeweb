Giới thiệu

Welcome to the Miss Universe Organization press site. This site is intended for members of the press covering MISS TEEN USA®, MISS UNIVERSE®, and MISS USA®. We have information for those who are planning to be on-location at one of our competitions and for those who will be covering us remotely.

On this site you will find high resolution images, high defintion videos, and press releases to aid you in your coverage.

You must apply for an account before you can access the high resolution photos and videos available on this site. If you wish to cover one of our Pageants in person, you must separately apply for on-site credentials.

This site is organized by Pageant. At the top of each page, a * appears over the currently selected Pageant. You can change Pageants at any time by clicking on Pageant at the top of the page. The "Newsfeed" section has the latest information, the "Press Kit" section contains background material, and the "Archives" contain material from our recent past. Detailed instructions are available on our help page.
